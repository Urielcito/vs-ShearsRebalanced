# vs-ShearsRebalanced
## Buffs shears to make them more viable when obtaining sticks, to compensate for the amount of sticks you can obtain with my other firewood to sticks mod.

### With this mod, normal leaves destroyed with shears will have an extra 25% chance of dropping a stick, and branchy leaves will have a 75% chance of dropping an extra stick, improving the average amount of sticks gathered per bush by approximately 50%-100%
